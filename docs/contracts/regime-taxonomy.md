# Approved Regime Taxonomy

**Version:** v1 — effective 2026-07-17 (owner-approved as the initial set; to be refined after the first live run. Changing this taxonomy re-triggers review of dependents.)

The fixed menu of regime classifications for the Market Regime Analyst. Every regime assessment classifies the current market as exactly ONE of these; free-form classification is prohibited (Market Regime Analyst design specification, D1).

| # | Regime | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Risk-On Uptrend** | Broad indices in established uptrends; volatility subdued or declining. |
| 2 | **Constructive but Choppy** | Indices rangebound or mildly higher; volatility moderate; no directional conviction. |
| 3 | **Elevated-Volatility Uptrend** | Indices still trending up, but volatility elevated or rising — environment risks are material despite the trend. |
| 4 | **Risk-Off Correction** | Indices below key trend measures; volatility elevated; breadth deteriorating. |
| 5 | **Volatility Shock** | Acute stress: volatility spiking, indiscriminate selling — environment risk dominates all other considerations. |

Classification is by preponderance of the observed evidence; where readings straddle two regimes, the analyst classifies to the more cautious one and records the tension in its Facts and Assumptions.
